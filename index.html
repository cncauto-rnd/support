Calculators

Calibration Factor Calculator

This widget takes four inputs and calculates the calibration factor for the FoldSafe controller.

Before moving the beam enter the encoder count shown in FoldSafe Diagnostic page. Then enter the initial beam height by measuring the distance between tip of the tool and the top of the die using a measuring device.

Repeat the procedure again after moving the beam arbitrary amount and enter the readings on After moving the beam section.
